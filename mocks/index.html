Technical Architecture diagram — Shape mocks

Three interactive mocks of the proposed second "Technical Architecture" diagram for the How It Works tab. Each shares the portal palette/typography. Click a flow chip to animate a request path; click any node for its contract & governance. Open each and compare.

Shape A

 Shape B
